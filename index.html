Privacy Policy for Zubkk App

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Zubkk app collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the Zubkk application.

Information Collection

Zubkk may collect the following information:

Eye Blink Detection:

The app utilizes the device's camera for eye blink detection. This is solely for the purpose of enabling scroll functions via eye blinking. The camera is used locally to process the eye movement and doesn’t transmit or store any images or sensitive data.

Accessibility Permission:

The app uses the accessibility permission to facilitate scroll functions without requiring touch input. This permission is specifically used for detecting forward scroll commands, enabled by eye blink detection. It does not access or collect any personal information or content from the user's device.

Device Permissions:

  • android.permission.CAMERA: This permission is utilized for eye blink detection and scroll functionality. It's solely used for the intended purpose and doesn’t capture or transmit any personal data.
  • android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS: This permission is used to provide necessary notifications related to the scroll function.
  • android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE: This permission enables the app to run essential services for the scroll functionality.
  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Utilized to ensure seamless connectivity for app functionality and updates.

Data Usage

Zubkk does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The data obtained through the camera and accessibility permission is processed locally within the app and is not shared with any third parties. No user data is stored on external servers.

Security

The app takes reasonable precautions to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of the user’s personal information, camera data, or accessibility permissions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Zubkk may update this privacy policy at any time. Users will be notified of any changes.

By using Zubkk, users signify their acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use Zubkk.
