Privacy Policy for Zbukk App

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the Zbukk app collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the Zbukk application.

Introduction

AquaCore technologies ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Zbukk app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our app.

Developer Information

  • Contact Email: aquacoretechnologies@gmail.com

Information Collection

Eye Blink Detection

The app utilizes the device's camera for eye blink detection. This is solely for the purpose of enabling scroll functions via eye blinking. The camera is used locally to process eye movement and doesn’t transmit or store any images or sensitive data.

Accessibility Permission

The app uses the accessibility permission to facilitate scroll functions without requiring touch input. This permission is specifically used for detecting forward scroll commands, enabled by eye blink detection. It does not access or collect any personal information or content from the user's device.

Device Permissions

  • android.permission.CAMERA: This permission is utilized for eye blink detection and scroll functionality. It's solely used for the intended purpose and doesn’t capture or transmit any personal data.
  • android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS: This permission is used to provide necessary notifications related to the scroll function.
  • android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE: This permission enables the app to run essential services for the scroll functionality.
  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Utilized to ensure seamless connectivity for app functionality and updates.

Data Usage

Zbukk does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The data obtained through the camera and accessibility permission is processed locally within the app and is not shared with any third parties. No user data is stored on external servers.

Security

The app takes reasonable precautions to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of the user’s personal information, camera data, or accessibility permissions. All data is processed locally and never transmitted over the internet.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since Zbukk does not collect any user data, there is no data retention or deletion policy required. Any data used is processed in real-time and not stored.

User Rights
